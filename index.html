About Me

I'm Shaik Abdul Ashiq, a passionate Software Developer and B.Tech ECE student at Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research, Chennai. My interests lie in Full Stack Development, IoT, and Artificial Intelligence. I enjoy transforming innovative ideas into real-world applications through coding, design, and problem-solving.

I've completed a Java Full Stack Development internship at EazyByts Infotech, where I gained hands-on experience in Spring Boot, RESTful APIs, and MySQL. Additionally, I worked as a Front-End Developer intern at Coursevita, where I built responsive web pages using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. I've also developed multiple personal projects such as a Clothing Rental Website and a Real Estate Management System.

As the ECE Project Leader and Class Representative, I've led technical teams, presented research papers at international conferences, and participated in Olympiad competitions. My recent innovations include projects on Smart Food Monitoring using AI, Automated Oil Extraction Machine using IoT, and Cricket Protein Extraction for Sustainable Nutrition.
